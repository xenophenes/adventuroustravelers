~ Active Adventures ~

These tours get your heart pumping; challenging your body and mind. These adventures are designed for the fit travelers looking to get a workout whilst exploring their destination.


San Diego - Multisport

Kayak 'The Jewel' on glassy morning waters of San Diego. Bike the beach and through luxurious neighborhoods ascend an aggressive hill worth the work for the views. Hike infamous Torrey Pines State Park learning about our land with our expert trail guide and return finishing the evening with a sunset dinner supporting a local business.

San Diego - WaterDaze

Snorkel to explore underwater mysteries of the San Diego coast. Kayak the Jewel and surf the infamous waves of the golden state.

Learn More

San Diego - Bike To Hike

Bike the meandering San Diego coast through luxurious neighborhoods whilst discovering hidden treasures then climbing up up and up a hill for beautiful vistas followed by a hike down to pristine sandy beaches and crystal blue waters where you will enjoy lunch and return hiking up and biking back into town.
