~ Adventure Itinerary~


Catalina Island


Come explore Catalina in a unique way through kayaking, camping, snorkeling, and hiking in a fully engaging trip from Thursday 9/22 - Sunday 9/25 for $250. Only have the weekends off? Live the experience from Friday 9/23 - Sunday 9/25 for only $214!

Projected Itinerary
(Itinerary is subject to change)


Thurs, 9/22

  • -Arrive at port of San Pedro at 8am (Carpools from SD can be arranged)
  • -Ferry to Two Harbors, Catalina Island
  • -Pick up last minute gear (camping fuel, any forgotten items)
  • -Kayak 4.5 miles NW (about 2hrs) to Parson's Landing
  • -Set up camp
  • -Explore/Hike North end of Island.


Frid, 9/23

  • -Pack up camp,
  • -Kayak back to Two Harbors to pick up other guests and additional supplies (water for next two days will need to be purchased).
  • -Kayak South to a prmitive camping site. (About 8-10miles total)
  • -Set up camp
  • -Snorkel/hike


Sat, 9/24

  • -Pack up camp
  • -Kayak and explore some see caves on the way to our next primitive camping site. (6-8 miles)
  • -Snorkeling


Sund, 9/25

  • -Enjoy a leisure morning snorkeling before packing up and kayaking back to Avalon to return kayaks. (4 miles)
  • -Enjoy exploring around Avalon before return ferry
  • -Meet ferry at 2pm for a 3pm departure back to San Pedro.

***What is included:

  • -Roundtrip ferries from San Pedro to Catalina
  • -Kayaks, life-jackets, paddles
  • -Campsites
  • -Permits
  • -Water at first campsite for first night (all other water needs to be packed in and can be purchased in Two Harbors on 2nd Day)

***NOT included:

  • -Snorkel package (snorkel mask, mouthpiece, fins, wetsuit) which can be rented for an additional $35/day pp or bring your own.
  • -Backpacking gear (Some of which can also be rented-REI.comdepending on what you need).
  • -Food
  • -WAG bags for proper waste disposal within primitive camping locations.

***Previous kayaking and backpacking experience is not required for the trip, though is highly recommended.


You will need to be in good physical condition to complete this journey. Days will include several hours of kayaking.
PM me for details if you are unsure of your capabilities so I can send you a plan to get you ready for this trip. That way you can fully enjoy the adventure. This will be an awesome experience and I hope to share it with you!

Please contact me directly should you have any questions regarding this adventure at 619-962-7622 or info@adventuroustravelers.net.
Once you are signed up, you will receive a confirmation from me with additional information regarding trip details as well as waivers.

Looking forward to adventuring with you!!
